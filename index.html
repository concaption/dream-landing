Frequently Asked Queries

Quick answers to common queries about our tool.

Verdict AI is an innovative tool that uses artificial intelligence to help individuals understand and navigate the legal world. It provides a personalized, user-friendly interface to simplify legal information.
Verdict AI works by analyzing your specific situation and presenting you with relevant legal articles, statutes, and precedents. It can also answer specific legal questions through its chat interface, eliminating legal jargon and making law easier to understand.
While Verdict AI is a powerful tool for understanding and navigating legal complexities, it is not a replacement for a lawyer. It can provide valuable insights and resources to help prepare you, but it is not equipped to provide legal advice or represent you in court.
